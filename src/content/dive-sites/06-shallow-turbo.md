## Shallow Turbo

Shallow Turbo is a wide, gently-sloping reef that tops out at 5 m and rarely exceeds 12. It's the kind of site that experienced divers tend to dismiss until they actually dive it — at which point it usually ends up on their list of favourites.

The shallow depth means light penetration is excellent, the coral cover is healthy, and the macro life is everywhere. Half a dive in nudibranchs alone is normal here.

**Marine life** — colourful reef fish, damselfish, wrasses, dozens of nudibranch species, seagrass beds with juvenile turtles, mantis shrimps if you're patient.

**Conditions** — calm, very shallow, excellent light.

**Best months** — year-round.

**Depth** — 5-12 m.

**Perfect for** — first dives, photography (especially macro), confidence-building after a long surface interval, and very relaxed second dives of the day.

**Tip** — bring a magnifier or a strong torch. The smallest things on this reef are also the most interesting.
