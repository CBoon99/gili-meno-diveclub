## Air Wall

Air Wall is the friendlier cousin of Meno Wall. Same wall structure, much gentler conditions: less current, slightly shallower, and packed with fish schools that hover in the water column like they're flying through air — hence the name.

The site is excellent for divers stepping up from beginner certification toward Advanced. Good practice for buoyancy in current, navigation along a wall, and the kind of slow, patient hover that gives you the best fish encounters.

**Marine life** — fusilier and snapper schools, groupers, turtles, lionfish in the cracks, occasional reef sharks at the deeper end, sweetlips at cleaning stations.

**Conditions** — moderate current, visibility 12-20 m, accessible depth.

**Best months** — April to November.

**Depth** — 8-30 m.

**Suitable for** — Advanced Adventurer students, Deep specialty students, and Open Water divers wanting their first taste of a wall dive in mild conditions.

**Highlights** — relaxing, fish-rich, easy to photograph. Slow descent past the schools, a long bottom phase along the wall, and a gentle drift back to the boat.
