## Deep Turbo

Deep Turbo is the same area as Shallow Turbo dropped over the edge. The site features a sandy plateau at 25 m broken up by large coral mounds — "bommies" — that rise from the sand. The mounds host their own miniature ecosystems: schooling fish, cleaning stations, and the larger pelagics that come in to use them.

This is one of the more reliable Gili Meno sites for shark sightings, and one of the only spots where we regularly see eagle rays cruising past in the open water beyond the mounds.

**Marine life** — large trevally, snappers, groupers, barracuda, white-tip and black-tip reef sharks, eagle rays in the blue, occasional Napoleon wrasse.

**Conditions** — variable current depending on tide, visibility 15-25 m, the deeper sections drop a few degrees in temperature.

**Best months** — May to October.

**Depth** — 15-45 m. We dive Advanced Adventurers and Deep Specialty divers only.

**Style** — explore mound by mound, watch into the blue between them, ascend with a long safety stop on the shallower plateau.

**Safety** — depth and current both matter here. Strict gas planning, conservative no-decompression limits, and stay in visual contact with your guide.
