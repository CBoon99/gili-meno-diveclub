## Meno Slope

Meno Slope is the workhorse intermediate site. A long, gradual slope from 8 m down to 30 m, blanketed in healthy hard and soft corals, with a steady stream of reef and pelagic fish moving through.

It's the site we use most for SSI Advanced Adventurer students working on Deep, Navigation, and Perfect Buoyancy adventure dives. The gentle gradient gives you a long bottom phase without ever feeling rushed; the topography is forgiving for navigation practice.

**Marine life** — groupers, snappers, trevally, turtles, eagle rays in the blue, white-tip reef sharks resting on the deeper sand patches, schooling fusiliers along the slope edge.

**Conditions** — moderate current, visibility 12-20 m.

**Best months** — May to October for the calmest days, but accessible year-round.

**Depth** — 10-30 m.

**Perfect for** — Advanced Adventurer students, Deep specialty students, and Open Water divers wanting their first dive past 18 m with an instructor.
