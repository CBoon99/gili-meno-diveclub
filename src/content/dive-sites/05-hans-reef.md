## Hans Reef

Hans Reef is a beginner's dream. Shallow, calm, and bursting with reef fish, this protected site on the south-east side of Gili Meno is where most of our Open Water Diver students do their first ocean dive. The coral cover is excellent, the visibility is reliably good, and the residents — parrotfish, wrasses, juvenile reef sharks, the occasional turtle — are unfazed by divers.

If conditions are rough at the more exposed sites, Hans Reef is usually still calm enough to dive. It's also the site we use most for refresher dives, photography practice, and Discover Scuba Diving experiences.

**Marine life** — parrotfish, wrasses, damselfish, juvenile reef sharks, lionfish, occasional turtles, octopuses at dawn and dusk.

**Conditions** — calm, shallow, very mild current.

**Best months** — year-round; especially good in the wet season when more exposed sites are choppy.

**Depth** — 5-20 m.

**Perfect for** — first ocean dives, skill drills, refresher dives, snorkellers visiting from the surface.

**Photography** — easy macro practice. Slow down, get low, find the small things.
