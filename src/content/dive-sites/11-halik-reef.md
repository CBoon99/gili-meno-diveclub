## Halik Reef

Halik Reef is one of the prettiest beginner sites on Gili Meno. A shallow coral garden with healthy hard coral cover, sandy patches, and consistently good visibility. The reef is shallow enough to be lit by direct sun for most of the day, which gives the colours an intensity you don't get on the deeper sites.

This is one of our favourite sites for the second open water dive of an Open Water Diver course — students have the basic skills sorted by then and can finally start *looking* at where they are.

**Marine life** — parrotfish, wrasses, surgeonfish, anthias clouds, juvenile turtles in the seagrass, small reef sharks at the deeper edge.

**Conditions** — calm, shallow, very mild current.

**Best months** — year-round.

**Depth** — 5-18 m.

**Perfect for** — first dives, certification dives, snorkellers, photographers chasing colour.
