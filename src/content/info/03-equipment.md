## What we provide

Every course and every fun-dive day includes the full set of equipment:

- **Tank** — 12-litre aluminium, filled to ~210 bar, all current hydro and visual
- **BCD** — current model, sized to fit
- **Regulator** — annual service, recent rebuild
- **Wetsuit** — 3 mm shorty for tropical comfort, full-length 3 mm in stock for thinner divers
- **Mask** — silicone, anti-fog treated, range of fits
- **Fins** — open-heel adjustable
- **Booties** — for boat work and reef entries
- **Weights and weight belt** — sized at the boat
- **Dive computer** — wrist-mount, current model, on every diver

This is included in every quoted price. There are no rental surcharges.

## What to bring

- **Swimwear and a change of clothes**
- **Reef-safe sunscreen** (or buy at the shop)
- **Long-sleeved sun shirt** for surface intervals
- **Light hat** — boat sun is fierce
- **Anti-seasickness tablets** if you're prone (we sell ginger tea on the boat for milder cases)
- **Your own log book** if you have one — otherwise we issue a digital one

If you have **a personal mask** (especially prescription), bring it. Mask fit is the most personal item in diving.

## Things you don't need to bring

- Tank, BCD, regulator (we provide)
- Wetsuit (we provide)
- Fins (we provide)
- Dive computer (we provide)
- Towels (the boat has them)
- Bottled water (we refill at the shop, free)

## When to consider buying

A common question: "should I buy my own gear?" Our honest answer:

- **Under 25 dives**: rent. You're still finding out what fits and what you like.
- **25-100 dives**: consider buying mask, fins, computer. These are the personal-fit items, and the rental cost adds up.
- **Over 100 dives**: it's probably time to buy a regulator and BCD too — particularly if you travel often.
- **Travelling lots**: a personal travel BCD (~3 kg packed) is worth its weight in baggage fees alone.

## Brands we use and trust

We rotate through a few main brands in our rental fleet:

- **Mares** — BCDs, regulators
- **Aqualung** — masks, fins
- **Scubapro** — older fleet items, very reliable
- **Suunto** and **Shearwater** — dive computers

If you're shopping for your own kit, any of those are reliable mid-range. We're not a retailer, but we're happy to give honest opinions on what would suit you.

## Servicing

If you're travelling with your own kit through Bali or Lombok, we can help find a reliable servicing shop. Annual regulator service is non-negotiable; everything else can be on a 2-year cycle.
