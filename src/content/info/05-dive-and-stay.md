## What "Dive & Stay" means

Three bookings in one: the dive course or fun-dive package, the accommodation, and (often) the boat transfer from Bali or Lombok. We deal with the partners, you deal with us.

It saves about an hour of planning per day, costs roughly the same as booking separately, and means we can usually get you a better rate at properties you couldn't get directly.

## Our partner properties

We work with a curated set of properties on Gili Meno (and a few on Gili Air for divers who want a slightly bigger island). Categories:

- **Beachfront bungalows** — simple, friendly, walking distance to the dive shop
- **Boutique villas** — pool, AC, breakfast included
- **Family rooms and lodges** — for the [Family Diving Package](/courses/family-package)
- **Eco-lodges** — solar power, low-impact construction, deeper into the island

We don't list the specific property names here — we'd rather match you to the right one based on your budget, group size, and what you're after. Tell us at booking what you'd like and we'll send three options.

## Sample packages

**3 nights + Open Water Diver**
- 3 nights mid-range bungalow accommodation
- Full SSI Open Water Diver course (3 days of diving)
- Daily breakfast
- All transfers from Bali or Lombok
- **From IDR 5,500,000** (~USD 340)

**5 nights + Open Water + 2 fun dives**
- 5 nights mid-range accommodation
- Full Open Water Diver course
- 2 additional fun dives
- Daily breakfast
- All transfers
- **From IDR 7,800,000** (~USD 480)

**7 nights + Advanced Adventurer + 4 fun dives**
- 7 nights with breakfast
- SSI Advanced Adventurer course
- 4 additional fun dives spread across the week
- All transfers
- **From IDR 9,500,000** (~USD 580)

**Family week (2 adults + 1 child)**
- 7 nights family room / villa
- [Family Diving Package](/courses/family-package)
- Daily breakfast
- All transfers
- Snorkel trip on a non-dive day
- **From IDR 16,500,000** (~USD 1010)

## What's actually included

- Accommodation in a partner property
- Daily breakfast (most properties)
- All diving as listed
- Boat transfers from Bali or Lombok
- Welcome drink at the dive shop
- WhatsApp link with us throughout your stay for any questions

## What you pay separately

- Lunches and dinners outside the dive shop
- Optional add-ons (private guide, photo package, additional fun dives)
- Travel insurance (mandatory, you arrange)
- Flights to Bali or Lombok

## Modifying a package

Any of the packages above can be tweaked:

- Swap nights for a longer course
- Add specialties or fun dives
- Upgrade to a villa or downgrade to a hostel
- Add a snorkel trip for non-diving partners
- Extend with a Komodo or Bali side trip — we have working relationships in both regions
