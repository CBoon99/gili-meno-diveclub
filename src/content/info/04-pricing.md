## Dive & Stay packages

We bundle accommodation with diving for divers wanting one bill, one booking. Pricing depends on the property; typical packages start at:

- **3 nights + Open Water Diver** — from IDR 5,500,000 (~USD 340)
- **5 nights + Open Water + 2 fun dives** — from IDR 7,800,000 (~USD 480)
- **7 nights + Advanced Adventurer + 4 fun dives** — from IDR 9,500,000 (~USD 580)

See the [Dive & Stay page](/dive-stay) for current options.

## What's NOT included

We're explicit about this so you don't get caught out:

- **Travel insurance** with diving cover (mandatory; you arrange)
- **Flights** to Lombok or Bali
- **Fast-boat transfers** Bali ↔ Gilis (~IDR 350,000 - 700,000 each way)
- **Public boat** Bangsal ↔ Gili Meno (~IDR 30,000 each way)
- **Meals** outside what's specifically called "lunch on the boat"
- **Accommodation** unless explicitly bundled
- **Optional extras** like prescription mask hire, photo packages, private guide

## Discounts we offer

- **Group of 4+**: 10% off course prices
- **Returning customer** (any prior course or 5+ fun dives): 10% off next course
- **Multi-course** (any two courses booked together): 15% off the second course
- **Long-stay fun divers** (10+ days, 10+ dives): negotiable, ask us
- **Volunteer DMTs** (Divemaster Trainees): course discounted; details on the [Divemaster page](/courses/divemaster)

## Payment

- **Cash** in IDR — preferred, ask for the discount in cash
- **Bank transfer** in IDR or USD
- **Card** with a 3% surcharge to cover bank fees (we lose money on cards otherwise)
- **Deposits** to hold a course date — typically 30%, refundable up to 14 days before

## Cancellation

- **More than 14 days** before start: full refund of any deposit
- **7-14 days** before: 50% refund
- **Within 7 days**: deposit retained; we'd rather reschedule you
- **Force majeure** (illness, flight cancellation, weather we can't dive in): we always reschedule or refund
