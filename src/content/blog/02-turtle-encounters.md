# Turtle Encounters at Gili Meno

One of the most magical experiences you can have while diving is swimming alongside sea turtles in their natural habitat. At Gili Meno, turtle encounters are virtually guaranteed—but there's a right way to do it.

## The Turtles of Gili Meno

We have two main sea turtle species here:

**Green Sea Turtles** (Chelonia mydas) — These gentle giants can live 50+ years and weigh up to 150kg. They're herbivorous and often seen grazing on sea grass beds.

**Hawksbill Sea Turtles** (Eretmochelys imbricata) — Smaller than greens, hawksbills have a distinctive pointed beak and are more active hunters.

## How to Interact Respectfully

**Maintain Distance**  
Keep at least 1 meter away. Turtles are shy—chasing them stresses them out and wastes your air.

**Never Touch**  
I know, you want to pet them. Don't. Touching removes their protective slime coating and can cause disease.

**Move Slowly**  
Swift movements scare turtles. Position yourself ahead of their path and let them approach.

**Take Wide Angle Photos**  
Get the turtle in their environment rather than a close-up of their shell.

**Protect Their Food**  
If a turtle is eating seagrass, leave them alone. Don't hover above them.

## Why Turtles Matter

Sea turtles are barometers of ocean health. They eat jellyfish (controlling populations), fertilize seagrass beds, and maintain healthy reef ecosystems. Their presence means a healthy underwater world.

## Conservation at Gili Meno

Gili Meno is a marine protected area partly due to turtle nesting. We partner with conservation groups to monitor nesting sites, protect hatchlings, and collect data on turtle health.

When you dive with us, a portion of your fee goes directly to turtle research and protection. You're not just having an amazing experience—you're helping save endangered species.

## The Experience

Respectful turtle encounters are deeply moving. Watching a turtle gracefully navigate the reef, seeing their ancient eyes, understanding their vulnerability—it changes how you view the ocean forever.

Come dive with us and meet the turtles of Gili Meno. Just remember: observe, don't disturb. 🐢
