## You're in good company

Roughly half our students arrive solo. People come to Gili Meno specifically because it's quieter than Bali or Trawangan, the dive school is small enough that you'll know everyone by your second day, and the routine of a multi-day course naturally pairs you with other divers at your level.

We don't do "solo supplements" or any of the surcharges that big resort dive shops sometimes apply. You pay the same as everyone else.

## Why solo divers do well here

- **Small classes**, max 4:1 ratio. Half the time you're the only student.
- **Buddy pairing**. Every dive day we pair solo divers with each other or with a guide. You never dive alone.
- **Daily routine**. Same boat, same crew, same students for the duration of your course. Friendships form quickly.
- **Single rooms** at most accommodation partners — sometimes at lower per-night rates than couples-rooms because they're smaller.

## What to expect socially

- **Mornings** — boat departs together; multi-day students get to know each other quickly
- **Lunch** — usually together at the dive shop or a nearby warung
- **Afternoons** — your own time. Beach, swim, cycle the island, sleep, work from a café
- **Evenings** — the dive school informally meets at one of the beachfront restaurants for dinner most nights. Optional but very common

## Routes for solo travellers

A few patterns we see often:

- **Five days at Gili Meno** then continue solo to Komodo or Raja Ampat with new diving friends from the course
- **Two-week stay**: Open Water then Advanced Adventurer, with fun-dive days in between
- **Working holiday**: a Divemaster internship over 6-12 weeks

## Safety solo

We take this seriously. Solo travellers worried about diving safety with a partner they don't know:

- Every dive briefing is done in detail, including roles and signals
- Buddy pairing is done before the boat leaves the harbour
- We never put a solo diver with a partner whose certification level is more than one tier different
- Guides accompany solo dives if no buddy match is available — at no extra cost
