## Diving with the family

Gili Meno is unusually well-suited to family diving. The reefs are shallow, the conditions are gentle, the island is car-free, and our pool is two minutes' walk from the harbour. Most importantly: every age in your family has a real, age-appropriate course we can run for them.

## What we offer per age

- **Ages 6-9**: SSI Scuba Rangers. Pool-only programme designed specifically for younger kids. Three sessions of skills in costume — fins, masks, "underwater archaeology", treasure hunts. They earn a certificate and a love of the water.
- **Ages 10-14**: Junior SSI Open Water Diver. Identical curriculum to the adult course, with shallower depth limits (12 m) and an adult buddy required. Full certification on completion.
- **Ages 15+**: Full SSI Open Water Diver. Adults and teenagers train together.

We also run **family snorkel trips** to Turtle City, including hand-held flotation devices for confident swimmers younger than the diving minimum age.

## Where to stay with kids

Gili Meno has accommodation specifically suited to families:

- Beachfront bungalows with pool (multiple operators)
- Family rooms at the eco-lodges in the centre of the island
- Self-catering apartments for families who'd rather cook some meals themselves

We can recommend properties our families have liked. Add it to your booking message.

## Practicalities

- **No motor vehicles on Gili Meno** — all transport is on foot, bike, or horse-drawn cart. Younger kids find this magical; older kids occasionally find it limiting.
- **Limited medical facilities** — Gili Meno has a basic clinic; serious medical needs go to Mataram on Lombok mainland (1 hour by speedboat). Travel insurance is essential.
- **Reef-safe sunscreen only.** We sell it at the shop if you forget.
- **Hydration matters more than you think.** Bring water bottles for everyone; refill at the shop.
