## Get back in the water — gently

If it's been a year, two years, ten years since your last dive — the easiest way back is a Scuba Skills Update. Half a day in our training pool, a relaxed lunch, then one guided dive at a calm site. You'll be surprised how much comes back.

This isn't a re-test. It's a refresh.

### What we cover

- Equipment setup and pre-dive checks (it's changed — but not as much as you think)
- Buoyancy and breathing — the core of comfortable diving
- Mask clearing, regulator recovery, ascents and safety stops
- A short brief on Gili Meno conditions, sites, and the marine life you might see
- Two open-water dives at one of our gentle reefs

### What's included

- All instruction with an SSI-certified instructor
- All equipment — tanks, weights, BCD, regulator, wetsuit, mask, fins
- Pool fees, boat transfer, and snacks
- A signed Scuba Skills Update card and updated logbook
- A no-pressure plan for what to do next

### What next?

Most refresher students continue with two or three fun dives over the following days. If you're feeling sharp and want to push further, we can roll the refresher straight into the **Advanced Adventurer** course at a discount.
