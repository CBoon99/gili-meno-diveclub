## Your gateway to the underwater world

The SSI Open Water Diver course is the first step toward becoming a confident, independent diver. Over three to four relaxed days you'll learn the theory online at your own pace, practise core skills in our training pool, and complete four open water dives on Gili Meno's friendliest reefs.

We keep groups small — a maximum of four students per instructor — so you get real attention, real practice time, and a real sense of achievement at the end.

### What you'll learn

- How to assemble, wear, and care for diving equipment
- Pressure, buoyancy, and the basic physics of being underwater
- The skills you need underwater: breathing, mask clearing, buoyancy control, equalisation, and emergency procedures
- How to plan a dive, navigate, and stay within safe depth and time limits
- How to dive responsibly: respecting marine life, avoiding contact with coral, and recognising the small things most divers miss

### Course structure

- **Day 1** — Online theory (you can start before you arrive) plus a confined-water session in our training pool. We focus on comfort and breathing first, skills second.
- **Day 2** — Two ocean dives at gentle, shallow sites such as Hans Reef or Halik Reef. Most students see their first turtle on day two.
- **Day 3** — Two more ocean dives covering the remaining skills and your certification dives. We tend to finish at Turtle City.

If you need an extra day to feel ready, you take it — there's no extra charge.

### What's included

- All instruction with an SSI-certified instructor
- All equipment: tanks, weights, BCD, regulator, wetsuit, mask, fins
- Online learning materials and digital SSI certification
- Boat transfers, drinking water, and snack between dives each day
- Digital logbook entry for every dive

### What to bring

- Swimwear and a towel
- Reef-safe sunscreen and a hat
- A light long-sleeved top for boat travel
- An open mind and patience with yourself
