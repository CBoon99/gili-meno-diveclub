## Choose your own diving adventure

The SSI Advanced Adventurer course is built around five "adventure dives" — each one is the first dive of a full SSI specialty. You pick the five that interest you, do them with an instructor, and finish certified. Two of them — **Deep** and **Navigation** — are required; the other three are yours to choose.

It's the fastest way to find out which kind of diving you actually love, and the cleanest path into deeper sites like Meno Wall, Deep Turbo and Bounty Wreck.

### Choose three adventures from

- **Boat diving** — boat etiquette, entries and exits in moving water
- **Drift diving** — riding currents along Gili Meno's walls
- **Night diving** — bioluminescence, sleeping turtles, and a completely different reef after dark
- **Wreck diving** — exploring the Bounty Wreck safely from the outside
- **Underwater photography** — your first proper underwater shoot
- **Perfect Buoyancy** — the most useful single dive most divers ever do
- **Search & recovery**, **Multilevel diving**, **Fish ID**, and more

We help you pick a combination that suits your interests and the conditions on the days you're with us.

### Course structure

- **Day 1** — Two adventure dives, including Deep
- **Day 2** — Three adventure dives, including Navigation, finishing with your favourite

You can spread it over more days if you want to fit in fun dives between the lessons.

### What's included

- All instruction with an SSI-certified instructor
- All equipment, tanks, weights, and boat transfers
- Online theory access and digital certification
- Five logged adventure dives, each one credited toward the matching specialty if you continue training
