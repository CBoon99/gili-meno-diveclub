## Make it the family trip everyone remembers

Diving is one of the few activities where a parent and a 12-year-old can have an exactly equal first experience. We built this package around that — kids and adults learning the same skills, in the same pool, then sharing the same first turtle on the same reef.

### How it works

- **Children aged 8-9** — start with **Scuba Rangers**, SSI's pool-only programme designed specifically for younger kids. Three pool sessions covering core skills with toys, games, and progress badges.
- **Children aged 10-14** — **Junior Open Water Diver**. Identical structure to the adult course, with shallower depth limits (12 m) and an adult buddy required.
- **Teens 15+ and adults** — **SSI Open Water Diver**. Full certification, dive together, dive forever.

### Suggested four-day flow

- **Day 1** — Pool sessions in age-grouped lessons (kids and adults learn separately, then come together for a snorkel)
- **Day 2** — More pool work plus a family snorkel at Turtle City for the whole group
- **Day 3** — First ocean dives. Kids and parents in the same boat, adjacent groups
- **Day 4** — Certification dives, lunch on the boat, certificates over ice cream

### What's included

- All instruction (we put extra instructors on for family groups)
- All equipment in junior and adult sizes
- Boat dives, snorkel sessions, transfers, snacks
- Digital certifications + a printed family certificate
- Photos throughout the four days
