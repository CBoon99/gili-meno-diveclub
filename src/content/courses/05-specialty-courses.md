## One specialty at a time

Specialty courses are the fastest way to add a real, practical skill to your diving and a credential to your card. Most run over one to two days with one classroom session and two ocean dives.

Stack three full SSI specialties + 24 logged dives and you earn the **Specialty Diver** rating. Five specialties + Stress & Rescue + 50 dives gives you **Master Diver**.

### Available specialties at Meno Dive Club

- **Deep Diving** — safely down to 30-40 m on Gili Meno's deeper sites
- **Night Diving** — sleeping turtles, hunting moray eels, and bioluminescence
- **Wreck Diving** — Bounty Wreck the safe way, from outside in
- **Underwater Photography** — your camera + buoyancy + light
- **Perfect Buoyancy** — the single most useful day in any diver's logbook
- **Drift Diving** — riding currents along Meno Wall and Air Wall
- **Boat Diving** — entries, exits, and the etiquette of working a boat
- **Search & Recovery** — patterns, lift bags, the unromantic but useful skills
- **Navigation** — natural and compass, day and night
- **Fish ID & Marine Ecology** — from "lots of fish" to "I saw three species of trevally"
- **Equipment techniques** — basic care and field troubleshooting

If you want a specialty we don't run on this list, ask — we can usually arrange it with our SSI Instructor Trainer.

### Course structure

Most specialties:

- **Day 1** — One classroom session and one ocean dive
- **Day 2** — Two ocean dives, the last of which counts as your assessment

### What's included

- All equipment for the relevant specialty
- All dives, boat transfers, and logbook entries
