## Your first professional rating

Becoming a Divemaster is more than a course — it's a working internship. You join the Meno Dive Club team, ride the boat every day, learn how a dive centre actually runs, and graduate with the skills, experience, and confidence to lead certified divers anywhere in the world.

### What you'll do

- **Skill demonstration** — every core skill, performed slowly enough that students can copy you
- **Dive theory** — physics, physiology, equipment, decompression, and the SSI standards
- **Dive leading** — guiding certified divers around our local sites, on your own briefings, with an instructor watching
- **Mapping** — produce a working map of one Gili Meno site
- **Search & recovery** — practical, planned, real
- **Workshops** — assisting on Open Water students, Discover Scuba experiences, and Stress & Rescue scenarios
- **Practicums** — running surface intervals, managing the boat, dealing with the small problems that happen on every dive day

Most internships run two to three weeks. We don't rush you through; we'd rather you graduate genuinely ready.

### What's included

- All instruction and SSI Divemaster certification on completion
- Crew kit (rashguard, club discount on equipment)
- All boat dives during the internship
- Mentoring from senior staff and instructors
- Help with your CV, references, and onward placement once certified

### Price

**IDR 8,500,000** (~USD 520) for the full internship. We can split this over two payments.

### Career path

Many of our Divemasters carry on into the **SSI Instructor Training Course (ITC)** — that step is run by SSI Instructor Trainers and we can introduce you to one when you're ready.
