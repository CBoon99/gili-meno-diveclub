## The course every diver remembers

Stress & Rescue is the most challenging — and most rewarding — recreational dive course you can take. You'll learn to spot a problem before it becomes an incident, manage your own stress underwater, and help a fellow diver in real difficulty.

It's also the moment your diving changes. You stop watching the reef and start watching your buddy too. You become the diver everyone wants on their boat.

### What you'll learn

- Recognising stress in yourself and other divers, on the surface and underwater
- Preventing problems through better planning, briefing, and self-awareness
- Self-rescue: cramps, out-of-air, lost buddy, equipment failures
- Tired, panicked, and unresponsive diver scenarios at the surface and at depth
- Managing a dive accident: oxygen administration, evacuation, paperwork
- Search patterns and navigation under stress

### Course structure

- **Days 1-2** — Theory, pool sessions, and confined-water rescue skills
- **Day 3** — Open-water scenarios at varying depths, including a simulated full rescue
- **Day 4** (if needed) — Final assessment scenario plus debrief over coffee

We deliberately leave time for honest, no-blame debriefs after each session — that's where most of the learning happens.
