## The biggest, busiest Gili

Gili Trawangan ("Gili T") is the largest and most developed of the three Gili Islands. It has the busiest beachfront, the biggest range of accommodation, and the loudest nightlife.

If Trawangan is where you want to stay, we pick you up. The boat ride to Gili Meno's dive sites is 10-15 minutes — barely longer than from Meno itself.

## Why base on Gili Trawangan

- Largest accommodation range — hostels through five-star resorts
- More restaurants, bars, and shops than the other two Gilis combined
- Full-time dental clinic, ATMs, larger pharmacies
- Day-tripping convenience to Bali, Lombok, and the other two Gilis

## Diving from Gili Trawangan

We pick up at Trawangan's main jetty at 7:15 AM daily. The sites are the same [13 named dive sites](/dive-sites) we run from Gili Meno; you can also explicitly book the Trawangan-side sites (Shallow Turbo, Deep Turbo) which are particularly close.

- **Pickup** — every morning at the main jetty, 7:15 AM
- **Pricing** — identical to courses booked from Gili Meno
- **Note** — day trips from Trawangan to Meno reefs avoid the busiest dive operators on Trawangan; you tend to have sites to yourself

## Getting to Gili Trawangan

- **Bali** — 2-3 hour fast-boats from Serangan, Padangbai, or Sanur
- **Lombok mainland (Bangsal)** — 25 minutes by public boat
- **Gili Meno or Gili Air** — 5-minute local hopper

## A note on quiet evenings

Trawangan is not the place to come for an early night. If you want full nights of sleep before early dive starts, we'd recommend Gili Meno or the quieter east side of Gili Air instead.
