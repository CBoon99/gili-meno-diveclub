## Lombok in a nutshell

Lombok is the large island **east of Bali** in **West Nusa Tenggara** — quieter, more rural, and dominated inland by **Mount Rinjani**. For most international divers, Lombok is a **transport hub**: you land at **LOP**, drive north to **Bangsal** or **Teluk Nara**, then hop on a boat to **Gili Meno**, **Gili Air**, or **Gili Trawangan**.

The **signature dive sites** of the Lombok Strait — [Turtle City](/dive-sites/turtle-city), [Meno Wall](/dive-sites/meno-wall), [Hans Reef](/dive-sites/hans-reef) — are **geographically off Lombok’s north-west coast** but are **driven from the Gili Islands** because boat times are minutes, not hours.

Flying from **Australia**? Our [**Australia → Indonesia diving**](/regions/australia-indonesia-diving) page covers visas, insurance, and typical routings via **DPS** or **LOP**.

## Lombok International Airport (LOP) → the Gilis

**Distance** — Bangsal harbour is roughly **1.5–2 hours** by car from LOP in normal traffic (longer in peak season or if there is a festival).

**Transfer options**

- **Private car** — pre-booked through your hotel or dive shop; most reliable with luggage and jet lag. Expect roughly **IDR 400,000–600,000** one-way (confirm current rates locally).
- **Airport taxi counter** — fixed-price tickets; fine for daytime arrivals.
- **Shared shuttle** — cheaper but slower; can work if you are not on a tight course start.

**Tip** — if your international flight lands after **15:00**, consider **one night in Senggigi or Mataram** and catch the public boat in the morning. Bangsal public boats stop in the evening; night crossings on unofficial boats are not worth the risk.

## Bangsal public boat vs private speedboat

**Public boat (bangsal → Gili Meno)** — cheap (roughly **IDR 15,000–35,000** depending on exact service and whether you are “local price” or tourist tariff), short (**~15–20 minutes** to Meno). You may wait until the boat fills. Tickets are bought at the harbour — ignore unofficial touts on the access road.

**Private speedboat charter** — faster and on your schedule; costs more (negotiate; prices vary by season). Some upscale Gili hotels include harbour pickup — ask when you book accommodation.

**Teluk Nara / Teluk Kode** — some resorts and charters use these bays **south-west of Bangsal**; same Gilis, different pier. Confirm which harbour your hotel or transfer company names on the voucher.

## Lombok mainland diving (vs the Gilis)

**Sekotong** (south-west Lombok) — relaxed muck and reef diving; fewer tourists; different operators from the Gili fleet.

**Belongas Bay** — advanced / seasonal sites; remote; not a beginner default.

**Gili Islands** — the **highest concentration** of operators, **easiest logistics**, and **most consistent** turtle and wall diving for typical holiday lengths.

We **only teach and guide** on the **Gili Meno** sites — if you want Sekotong or Belongas, we can recommend partner shops, but our courses and fun dives are run from **Meno Dive Club** on **Jl. Gili Meno**.

## Safety: chamber and evacuation

Indonesia’s **hyperbaric chamber network** is thinner than Australia’s. The nearest chamber used by Gilis operators is on **Lombok mainland (Mataram area)** — roughly **an hour by speedboat** from Gili Meno in good conditions. We carry **oxygen on board**, maintain **radio contact**, and require **dive insurance** that covers treatment and evacuation — see our [diving safety notes](/info/diving-safety) and the [Australia hub](/regions/australia-indonesia-diving) for insurance wording.

## One night on Lombok before the Gilis

**Senggigi** — beach strip north of Mataram; plenty of hotels; **45–60 minutes** to Bangsal by car.

**Mataram** — capital; business hotels; good if you arrive late and need a sure sleep before boats.

**Kuta Lombok** (south) — surf town; **not** near Bangsal — only stay here if you are deliberately touring the south before heading north (add **2+ hours** drive to Bangsal).

## Onward to the Gilis — pick your island

- **[Gili Meno](/regions/gili-meno)** — quietest; our home; turtles and small-school vibe.
- **[Gili Air](/regions/gili-air)** — balance of restaurants and calm.
- **[Gili Trawangan](/regions/gili-trawangan)** — largest; nightlife and most boat departures.

Whichever you sleep on, you can still **dive with us** — we meet you at **Meno harbour** or arrange boat timing with you in advance.

## Bali ↔ Lombok without flying

Some travellers take a **fast boat Bali → Gili** directly (see [Bali → Gili Meno](/regions/bali)); others fly **DPS → LOP** for time savings. Both are valid — match the option to your **tolerance for sea time vs airport hassle**.
