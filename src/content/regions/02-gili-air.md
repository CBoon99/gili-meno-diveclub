## The friendly middle Gili

Gili Air is the closest of the three Gili Islands to Lombok and the most permanently inhabited. It has the best café scene of the three, more long-stay residents, and a slightly more "lived-in" feel than its neighbours.

The diving from Gili Air is identical to the diving from Gili Meno — the boat ride to most sites takes roughly the same time, and the underwater landscapes are the same shared coral plateau. We pick guests up from Gili Air's main dock for free if you're staying there for your course or fun-dive package.

## Why base on Gili Air

- More accommodation choice — backpacker hostels, boutique resorts, family bungalows
- Stronger café and restaurant scene
- Best yoga and wellness offerings of the three islands
- Daily boats to Bali, Lombok, and Gili Meno

## Diving from Gili Air

Same boat, same sites, same instructors. Pick from any course on the [main courses page](/courses), and tell us at booking that you're staying on Gili Air.

- **Pickup** — every morning at the main dock, 7:30 AM
- **Sites** — same 13 [dive sites](/dive-sites)
- **Pricing** — identical to courses booked from Gili Meno

## Getting to Gili Air

- **Lombok mainland** — 15 minutes from Bangsal harbour
- **Bali** — 2-3 hour fast-boat from Padangbai or Serangan
- **Gili Meno or Gili Trawangan** — 5-minute local boat-shuttle

## Beyond diving

- Sunrise on the east side
- Snorkelling at the harbour-side patch reef (turtles common)
- The "island loop" — 2-hour walk or cycle around Gili Air
- Cafés, yoga, and the island's small night market
